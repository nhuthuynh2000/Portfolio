Quiz Master

"Ask and answers, a trivia quiz game that features one question and four answer choices. At the start of the game, random questions are selected from a pool of Scriptable Objects containing various questions."

"In this project, I have implemented a countdown timer; if players do not answer in time, they lose the game. This feature adds an extra layer of excitement and urgency to the gameplay."

"Throughout this project, I have learned to effectively use Scriptable Objects, honed my object-oriented programming skills, created a user interface, and explored many other new concepts in game development. This experience has greatly enhanced my understanding of building engaging games and managing game state. I am excited to continue developing my skills and creating more interactive projects in the future!"
