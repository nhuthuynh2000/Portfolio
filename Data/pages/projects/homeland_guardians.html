Homeland Guardians - Unity 6 (In Progressing)

"I have a game that is a click RPG similar to VLTK, Kiếm Thế, and Diablo. I am utilizing the latest technology in Unity 6, featuring many new mechanics such as Animator Override, Saving System, Change Weapon, and more.

I aim to provide players with an exciting adventure experience, allowing them to decide the direction of their character, whether they want to be a powerful wizard or a brave warrior. There will be a quest system to help players learn more about the world, as well as challenging enemies and bosses with various mechanics."
