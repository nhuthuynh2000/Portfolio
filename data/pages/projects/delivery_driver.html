Delivery Driver

"My delivery Driver offers an exciting experience for players as they control a delivery car in a colorful world. In the game, when the car passes over a pink square, it changes color to pink and enters a delivery state. Players must drive to a blue square to deliver the goods before returning to pick up more from the pink square."

"On the map, there are yellow triangles; whenever the car passes over one, it will accelerate until it collides with an obstacle. This creates thrilling moments and encourages players to seek out the yellow triangles to improve their delivery performance. However, when colliding with obstacles such as houses, rocks, or trees, the car will slow down if it is in a state of acceleration. This requires players to be skillful in their control to avoid collisions while maximizing their speed to complete delivery tasks."

"A unique aspect of the game design is the absence of a health bar. This truly helps players feel relaxed and comfortable during gameplay. Instead of worrying about losing lives or scores, players can focus entirely on controlling the car and completing their delivery tasks. This feature creates a light-hearted gaming environment that encourages creativity and experimentation in gameplay."

I am thrilled to see that this project not only brings joy to players but also helps me explore game design and create interesting and innovative gameplay mechanics. This is my first Unity project, and this experience has deepened my understanding of the game development process. I hope the game will provide relaxing and enjoyable moments for all players, while also serving as a stepping stone for my future game projects!
